Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

The App Lockr (the "app") is built and published by Ubersoft ("we," "us," "our"). This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: your passwords stay on your device, and we can't see them — the app has no way to send them to us.

In plain terms
  • No account. No sign-up, no login, no profile.
  • No servers. Your logins, passwords, and notes live only on your device, encrypted.
  • No network permission. The app cannot access the internet at all — you can verify this yourself.
  • Nothing reaches us. We can't see, recover, or reset your vault, because it never leaves your device.

01 Information the app stores on your device

The app lets you save login entries — site or app names, usernames, passwords, and notes — plus your preferences such as theme and auto-lock timing. All of it is encrypted with your master password and stored in a single vault file on your device. It is not transmitted to us or to anyone else.

02 Information we collect

We do not collect, receive, or store any information about you. The app has no account system, no server component, and — unusually for a modern app — requests no internet permission at all, so it has no technical means of sending your data anywhere. You can confirm this in your device's app settings.

03 Backups and exports

The app lets you export your vault as a backup file. That file is created on your device at your request, stays encrypted with your master password, and is saved to a location you choose. We do not receive a copy. If you later move, share, or store that file somewhere else, it is handled under the terms of whatever service or location you select — and it remains unreadable without your master password.

04 Analytics and advertising

The app contains no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, and no third-party tracking technologies. We do not build a profile of you, and the app does not use advertising identifiers.

05 Purchases

The App Lockr is a one-time paid app. Your purchase is processed by Google Play, not by us — we never see or store your payment details. Google's handling of your purchase and account information is governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

06 Permissions

The app requests only what it needs to work on your device:

  • Biometrics (optional): if you turn on fingerprint or face unlock, the check is performed entirely by your device's own secure hardware. Biometric data never enters the app and never leaves your device.
  • Autofill service (optional): if you set The App Lockr as your autofill provider, Android shows your matching logins inside other apps and browsers. The matching happens on your device; nothing is sent anywhere.
  • Storage access (where applicable): used only to let you save or load an encrypted backup file that you create.

Notably absent: internet access. The app does not request it and cannot use it.

07 Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect information from children. Because the app collects no information at all, no personal information from any user — child or adult — is gathered by us.

08 Your choices and rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on our side. You remain in full control of the information on your device: you can edit or delete any entry in the app, clear the app's data from your device settings, or uninstall the app to remove everything. Depending on where you live — for example under the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA — you may have rights regarding personal data a company holds about you. We hold none.

09 Data security

Your vault is encrypted on your device using strong, industry-standard cryptography, with a key derived from your master password and protected by your device's secure hardware. Because we never hold your vault or your master password, we cannot recover either one — if you lose both your master password and your device without a backup, your vault is unrecoverable by design. We recommend keeping your device locked, its software up to date, and an exported backup somewhere safe. There is no central database of anyone's passwords for an attacker to breach.

10 Third-party services

The app is distributed through Google Play and uses Google Play to process payment. Apart from that, the app does not send your information to any third party — it has no network permission with which to do so.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll update the effective date above and post the new version at theapplockr.com/privacy. Continuing to use the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

12 Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@ubersoft.pro.
