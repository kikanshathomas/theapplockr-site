Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: 13 JULY 2026

The App Lockr is made by Ubersoft. This is the shortest honest privacy policy we could write, because there is very little to say.

What we collect

Nothing. The App Lockr has no account system, connects to no server, and requests no INTERNET permission on your device. We cannot collect your data because the app has no way to send it to us.

Your passwords and vault

Everything you store — logins, usernames, passwords, notes — is encrypted and kept in a single file on your device. It never reaches us or any third party. We have no ability to read, recover, or reset it.

Analytics and tracking

There are none. No analytics SDKs, no crash-reporting services, no advertising identifiers, no third-party code that phones home. If the app ever records a diagnostic log, it stays on your device and is only ever shared if you choose to export it.

Backups

If you export a backup, the resulting file is created on your device and saved wherever you send it — for example your own storage or a location you pick. That file is encrypted and never passes through us.

Purchases

The App Lockr is a one-time paid app on Google Play. Any payment is handled entirely by Google under Google's terms and privacy policy; we never see your payment details. We receive only the anonymous aggregate sales information Google provides to all developers.

Children

The app is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children. It collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. The core promise — nothing leaves your device — will not change; it's the reason the app exists.

Contact

Questions? Reach Ubersoft support at support@ubersoft.pro.
